Flavorful Pilau Recipe.

Pilau is a flavorful rice dish with Middle Eastern roots that's a staple in East African cuisine.
It's a fragrant rice dish cooked with a mix of aromatic spices like cumin, cardamom, and cinnamon.
The rice is saut√©ed with lots of onions;bulb onions to be precise until they turn golden brown, garlic, and ginger to build a rich base.
Meat (such as chicken, beef, or goat) and peeled irish potatoes are then added for a complete meal.
The dish is slow-cooked, allowing the flavors to meld and the rice to absorb the spices, resulting in a deliciously aromatic and satisfying meal.
The dish can be served with a side of guacamole, banana and salsa

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati rice.
  • 3 large red onions.
  • 2 tbsp crushed garlic.
  • 2 tbsp crushed ginger.
  • 3 tbsp Pilau masala.
  • Half a kg of beef chopped
  • 5 large potatoes, peeled.
  • A cup vegetable oil.
  • 4 cups of water/beef stock.
  • Salt to taste.

Procedure.

  1. In a large pot add your chopped meat, beef stock or water , any spice of your choice and salt to taste. Boil the meat until its done(not tough or too soft)
  2. Ater the meat is done, pour the remaining stock in a separate container and leave the meat in the pot. Place the pot on the stove and add oil to brown the meat, sautee the meat until its brown and place it aside.
  3. In a separate pot, add your chopped onions and a cup of oil and sautee the onions until they are golden brown.
    Add the crushed ginger garlic and the masala until it forms a thick brown paste.
    Add the potatoes and meat and stir fry them in the paste until they are well coated.
  4. Finally, add the rice and add four cups of water, taste for salt and adjust according to taste.
    Cover and cook under low heat until the rice is cook and fuffy .
  5. Serve hot with a side of salsa, guacamole and a ripe banana.
  6. Enjoy!
