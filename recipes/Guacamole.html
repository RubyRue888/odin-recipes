Guacamole Recipe

Creamy Guacamole.

This guacamole is like no other.
It's fresh, tasty and creamy all at the same time.
Made from the finest Ingredients, it brings flavor and let's not forget it's healthy too.
It's made using the creamiest avocado variety; Hass avocado and fresh tomatoes, onions and lime to add acidity

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe hass avocados
  • 2 ripe plump tomatoes diced
  • 1 red/whit onion diced
  • Half a cup of lime juice
  • Salt to taste.

Procedure.

  1. Use a spoon to scoop up the flesh of your halved avocado and place it in a bowl andthe mash it.
  2. Add your diced tomatoes and onions(soack the diced onions in warm water for 10 minutes to get rid of the punget onions flavor before adding to the salsa)
  3. Add the lime juice and salt according to your taste, mix them until they are uniformly mixed.
  4. Enjoy!
