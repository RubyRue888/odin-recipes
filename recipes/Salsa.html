Fresh Salsa Recipe

Salsa is a versatile dish that can accompany most if not all dishes. It's easy to make and brings freshness and flavor.
With only only five simple ingridients, one can make this wonderful side.
It's also known as Pico de Gallo.

Ingredients

  • 3 plump ripe tomatoes diced
  • 1 big red or white onion diced.
  • A sprig of corriander
  • Half a cup lime juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 Jalapenos (optional)

Procedure

  1. In a bowl, add your diced onions and add a pinch of salt and warm water. Soack for ten minutes and drain the water.
  2. Ater draining the water, add your diced tomatoes and finely chopped corriander.
  3. Add the chopped jalapenos according to your preference (optional).
  4. Add the lime juice and salt to taste.
  5. Mix and Enjoy!
